My Rents

Do you have different rents to manage? Tenants information, contracts, pictures, events, details... It can be difficult. With My Rents you can easily manage all of this creating different portfolios with their rents and have a better control of your earnings and expenses.

Party Words

Game to play with family or friends in which you will represent words through gestures or draws. Only you will need is a mobile to play with. You can create custom sets or choose one of the already created ones.

Profits

Salary, mortgage, bills, savings, investments, payments... there are too many of them and it can be stressful. With Profits you can have all of them together and see how much money you are making or losing in real time without extra calculations. You can also see a global balance of everything together. You cannot save money with us... but at least save some time.
